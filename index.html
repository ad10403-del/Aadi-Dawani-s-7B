Do video games actually help the brain?

Benefits of video games

  • Improved attention and focus Playing action based video games in particular, can train the brain to better focus on relevant information and ignore distractions.
  • Faster information processing Video games can improve the speed of information processing by demanding players to constantly interpret novel data, adapt strategies, and make decisions in dynamic, interactive environments.
  • Improved hand-eye cooridinations and reaction times The need for quick, precise movements in many games hones motor skills, reflexes and brain structures.

Negatives of video games

  • Addiction Video game addiction
